1. PAY CYCLE

All Meridian employees are paid on a bi-weekly basis, with payroll processed every other Friday. If a scheduled payday falls on a bank holiday, payment is issued on the preceding business day. Pay stubs and tax documents are accessible through the ADP self-service portal at all times.

Employees may enroll in direct deposit (strongly encouraged) or opt to receive a physical check. Changes to banking information must be submitted to payroll@meridiantech.com at least 5 business days before the next pay cycle.

2. HEALTH INSURANCE

Meridian provides comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage through our benefits partners:

Medical: Employees are covered at 100% company-paid premiums for individual coverage. Dependent coverage (spouse/domestic partner and children) is available at 20% employee contribution of the premium cost. Three plan tiers are available: High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) with HSA, PPO Standard, and PPO Premium.

Dental: Full individual coverage is company-paid. Dependent dental coverage is available at 20% employee cost contribution.

Vision: Full individual and dependent vision coverage is company-paid.

Benefits enrollment opens during the annual open enrollment window each November and takes effect January 1 of the following year. New hires may enroll within 30 days of their start date.

3. 401(k) RETIREMENT PLAN

Meridian offers a 401(k) plan administered by Fidelity. Key details:

- Company match: 4% of base salary (dollar-for-dollar match up to 4% of compensation)
- Vesting schedule: 2-year cliff vesting (employees become 100% vested in company match after 2 years of service; no partial vesting prior to that date)
- Employees may contribute up to the IRS annual maximum
- Roth 401(k) contributions are also available
- Enrollment is available from day one of employment

4. STOCK OPTIONS

Equity is a meaningful part of total compensation at Meridian. All full-time employees are granted stock options upon hire. Standard terms:

- Grant vesting schedule: 4-year vesting with a 1-year cliff (25% vests after year one, then monthly thereafter)
- Exercise price: set at fair market value on the grant date
- Options expire 10 years from the grant date, or 90 days after departure
- Additional equity refresh grants may be awarded in connection with promotions or strong performance (discretionary, subject to board approval)

Employees are encouraged to review their option grant agreements in the Carta equity portal and consult with a personal financial advisor regarding tax implications.

5. TIME OFF

Paid Time Off (PTO): 20 days per year (accrued at 1.67 days per month). Unused PTO up to 5 days may be rolled over to the following year.

Sick Leave: 12 sick days per year (not accrued; available at the start of each calendar year). Sick leave does not roll over.

Federal Holidays: Meridian observes 10 federal holidays per year. The holiday schedule is published annually on the HR intranet.

Bereavement Leave: Up to 5 days paid leave for the loss of an immediate family member; 2 days for extended family.

6. LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT BUDGET

Each full-time employee receives $2,000 per calendar year to spend on professional development, including:
- Online courses and certifications
- Professional conferences and workshops
- Books and learning resources

Requests are submitted via the Learning & Development portal on the HR intranet. Expenses are reimbursed within 30 days of submission with appropriate receipts and manager approval.

7. WELLNESS REIMBURSEMENT

Meridian offers a $500 annual wellness reimbursement to support employee health and wellbeing. Eligible expenses include gym memberships, fitness classes, mental health apps, ergonomic equipment, and similar wellness-related costs. Submit receipts through the expense portal under the "Wellness" category.

8. EMPLOYEE REFERRAL PROGRAM

Meridian encourages employees to refer qualified candidates from their networks:

- Referral bonus for senior-level roles (Staff Engineer, Senior Manager, and above): $3,000
- Referral bonus for all other roles: $1,500

Bonuses are paid after the referred employee completes 90 days of employment. Employees in recruiting or HR roles directly involved in the hiring decision are not eligible for referral bonuses on that specific hire.

9. EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (EAP)

All employees and their household members have access to Meridian's Employee Assistance Program at no cost. The EAP provides confidential support for:

- Mental health counseling (up to 8 free sessions per year with licensed therapists)
- Financial planning and debt counseling
- Legal consultation services
- Childcare and eldercare referral resources

The EAP is accessible 24/7 by calling 1-800-555-0147 or visiting eap.meridiantech.com. All interactions are strictly confidential and not reported to Meridian.

10. MANAGER-LEVEL ADDITIONAL BENEFITS

Employees at the Manager level and above receive the following additional benefit:

- Executive coaching budget: $5,000 per year for certified coaching engagements

Additional details on manager and executive compensation structures, including bonus targets and equity refresh schedules, are documented in the Manager Compensation Guide (access level: manager). Contact your HR Business Partner to request access.
